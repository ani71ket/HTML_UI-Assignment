Can I take this at night?

+

Yes, Alcami Elements can be taken at night without disrupting sleep. However, its focus and energy-boosting ingredients may suit daytime use better for some. If sensitive to supplements, take it earlier. Consult a healthcare provider if needed.

Where are your ingredients sourced from?

+

Our ingredients are sourced globally for quality and sustainability: Lion’s Mane from the Pacific Northwest, Ashwagandha from India, Rhodiola from Siberia, Bacopa Monnieri from Southeast Asia, and L-Theanine from Japan. All are rigorously tested for purity.

Will this get me high?

+

No, Alcami Elements won’t get you high. It uses non-psychoactive adaptogens and nootropics like Lion’s Mane and Ashwagandha to enhance cognition and relaxation safely. It’s designed to support daily well-being without mind-altering effects.

Are you using fruiting bodies for your mushrooms?

+

Yes, we use 100% fruiting bodies in our mushrooms, like Lion’s Mane, for maximum cognitive benefits. Rich in bioactive compounds like beta-glucans, they offer higher potency than mycelium-based products, ensuring effectiveness without fillers.
